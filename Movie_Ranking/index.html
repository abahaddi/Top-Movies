The Best Movies According to Amin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

13 Hours

I like 13 Hours because it is based on a true story and shows intense real-life events in a very realistic way, without exaggeration.

I also like the immersive atmosphere of the film, which makes me feel like I am right in the middle of the action with the soldiers.

I like the constant tension and the highly realistic combat scenes, which keep the movie engaging from start to finish.

Finally, I like the message of courage and teamwork between the soldiers facing extreme danger together.

Ex Machina

Really cool sci-fi movie.

Drive

Super beautiful film. Really artistic.
